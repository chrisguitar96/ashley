what's new?

Ashley just finished her
major project for her masters at RBC, which entailed writing and
performing a one-woman show.

"Protests Too Much"

A modern adaptation of one of Shakespeare’s greatest
tragedies that explores questions about language, voice, and
feminism. Flipping between Hamlet and the Ghost of her
father, the show mixes the Bard's text with contemporary
Female pioneers in poetry: Leland Bardwell, Tess Gallagher,
Louise Glück, Amanda Gorman, Jane Hirshfield, Denise
Levertov, Audre Lorde, Sylvia Plath, & Maya C. Popa.
